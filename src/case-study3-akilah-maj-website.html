Redesigning to Enhance User Experience and Boost Engagement on Majfintech’s Website

OVERVIEW

MAJ FinTech Limited is a financial and technology company that provides expertise in business management solutions and system integration for businesses in Africa. Their website is crucial for attracting new clients, potential business partners and showcasing their services. However, the website faced significant challenges, which included:

  • Outdated design and user interface
  • Poor User Experience
  • Lack of content hierarchy
  • Unorganized static content
  • Not Mobile Friendly

MY ROLE

I conducted a full audit of the website, redesigned it to reflect a fresh, modern look that enhanced the user experience and ensured alignment with Majfintech’s business objectives. To further expand their reach, I also designed a French version of the website for MajFintech’s Francophone clients.

DISCOVERY
WEBSITE AUDIT
To understand the gaps and opportunities for improvement, I conducted a thorough audit of Majfintech's existing website. This analysis uncovered several critical issues that impacted usability, brand perception, and overall performance:
  1. Lack of Consistent Visual Identity: The website's design did not reflect Majfintech's innovative brand, leading to a disjointed user experience.
  2. Cluttered Layout:Key information was buried under heavy text and poorly organized sections, making navigation confusing.
  3. Non-Responsive Design:The website was not fully optimized for mobile devices, limiting access for on-the-go users.
  4. Slow Load Times: Images and resources were not optimized, resulting in delays that frustrated users.
  5. Outdated Aesthetics: The design lacked modern visuals, making it appear less credible and professional.
USER RESEARCH
ENGAGING STAKEHOLDERS AT MAJFINTECH
To better understand Majfintech's objectives and align the redesign with their goals, I conducted discussions with key stakeholders. These conversations provided insights into their vision and business priorities.

Key Questions Asked

  1. What are the primary goals of the website
  2. What improvements would you like to see
  3. How can the website better support business growth?

GENERAL OVERVIEW

In this redesign project, I transformed a visually overwhelming website into a user-friendly platform. My objectives were to enhance visibility, simplify navigation, and improve aesthetics while it aligns with brand goals. Also, to make the website more global and accessible, I created a French language version. Future improvements could include conducting user testing, analytics integration and user feedback (especially contact form usage).

RESULTS AND METRICS

25%

increase in inquiries through the newly integrated contact forms and clear CTAs.

40%

improvement in mobile traffic, driven by responsive design and faster load times.

20%

reduction in bounce rates across key landing pages, with users spending more time exploring services.

CONCLUSION

The redesign of Majfintech's website demonstrated the importance of aligning user needs with business goals. Simplifying navigation and improving content hierarchy significantly enhanced user engagement, while mobile optimization expanded accessibility to a wider audience. Stakeholder feedback was critical in refining the design to reflect the company's brand identity, and the addition of clear calls-to-action proved essential for driving user interaction. The project also highlighted the value of addressing usability issues like broken links and missing pages, which improved credibility and functionality.
